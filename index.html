PUBLIC ADVISORY

In support of the energy conservation measures under Office of the President Memorandum Circular No. 114, s. 2026, the Philippine Reclamation Authority (PRA) will implement a four (4)-day onsite work arrangement from 8:00AM to 5:00PM

Effective 09 March 2026, PRA offices will operate onsite from Monday to Thursday, while Fridayswill be observed as a Work-From-Home (WFH) day for all personnel, including those from the Records Section and Cashier.

During Fridays, all incoming communications, requests, and inquiries may be sent through the PRA official email address: info@pea.gov.ph

Furthermore, payments can be made through the Landbank Link.BizPortal.

We advise our stakeholders to plan their transactions with PRA in accordance with this adjusted work arrangement.

For the information and guidance of the public.
